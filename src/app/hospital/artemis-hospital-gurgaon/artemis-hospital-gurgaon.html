Established in 2007, Artemis Hospital is a leading multi-specialty hospital located in the heart of Gurgaon. It is the first JCI (Joint Commission International) and NABH-accredited hospital in the city, known for its commitment to providing top-notch healthcare services. Spread across 9 acres, Artemis Hospital offers a harmonious blend of cutting-edge technology, clinical excellence, and compassionate care.

The hospital is designed to provide a patient-friendly environment while delivering advanced medical treatments. It is home to highly skilled doctors, state-of-the-art infrastructure, and a multidisciplinary approach to care, ensuring that patients receive the best outcomes. Over the years, Artemis has earned a stellar reputation as one of the most trusted healthcare destinations, attracting patients from India and across the globe.

Specialties and Services:

Artemis Hospital offers a comprehensive range of specialties, delivering quality care for a variety of medical conditions. Its key areas of expertise include:

  • Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery: Advanced diagnosis and treatment for heart diseases, including minimally invasive procedures.
  • Oncology: A specialized cancer care program with facilities for medical, radiation, and surgical oncology.
  • Neurology and Neurosurgery: Comprehensive care for brain, spine, and nervous system disorders, using the latest techniques.
  • Orthopedics and Joint Replacement: Expertise in managing joint problems, sports injuries, and trauma care.
  • Gastroenterology and Hepatology: Specialized treatment for digestive and liver disorders.
  • Transplant Program: Recognized for successful liver, kidney, and bone marrow transplants.

Facilities and Technology:

Artemis Hospital is equipped with world-class infrastructure and advanced medical technologies to ensure precision in diagnosis and treatment. Some of its notable facilities include:

  • Over 400 beds, with dedicated ICUs and critical care units.
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic and imaging services.
  • Robotic-assisted surgeries and minimally invasive procedures.
  • Comprehensive rehabilitation and wellness programs.

Accreditations and Certifications:

Artemis Hospital is recognized for its adherence to global standards in healthcare, ensuring patient safety and quality care. Key accreditations include:

  • JCI (Joint Commission International): Internationally recognized certification for world-class healthcare services.
  • NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers): Acknowledged for maintaining the highest quality standards.
  • NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories): Certified for excellence in laboratory diagnostics.
