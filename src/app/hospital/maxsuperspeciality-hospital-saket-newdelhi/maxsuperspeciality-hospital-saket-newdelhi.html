Max Super Speciality Hospital, Saket, is one of the most prestigious healthcare institutions in New Delhi. Established in 2006, it is a state-of-the-art tertiary care hospital offering comprehensive medical services under one roof. Renowned for its patient-first approach, the hospital is staffed by a team of highly skilled medical professionals and equipped with advanced technology to deliver world-class healthcare.

Spread across two wings, the East and West blocks, Max Hospital Saket offers a serene and patient-friendly environment. It is well-known for its holistic approach to treatment, combining modern medical advancements with compassionate care. Over the years, it has become a trusted name in healthcare, attracting patients from across India and around the globe.

Specialties and Services:

Max Super Speciality Hospital provides an extensive range of specialties, ensuring personalized care for a variety of medical needs. Key areas of expertise include:

  • Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery: Advanced care for heart conditions, including minimally invasive and robotic cardiac surgeries.
  • Oncology: Comprehensive cancer care, with dedicated centers for radiation, medical, and surgical oncology.
  • Neurology and Neurosurgery: Expertise in treating neurological disorders and performing complex brain and spine surgeries.
  • Orthopedics and Joint Replacement: Specialized care for joint replacement, sports injuries, and orthopedic trauma.
  • Gastroenterology and Hepatology: Advanced treatment for digestive, pancreatic, and liver disorders.
  • Transplant Program: Recognized for successful liver, kidney, and bone marrow transplants.

Facilities and Technology:

Max Hospital Saket is equipped with cutting-edge infrastructure and technology to ensure precise diagnosis and effective treatment. Key facilities include:

  • Over 500 beds, including ICUs and critical care units.
  • Advanced robotic surgery systems for minimally invasive procedures.
  • Comprehensive diagnostic services, including PET-CT, MRI, and digital X-ray.
  • Specialized rehabilitation centers for post-treatment care and recovery.

Accreditations and Certifications:

Max Super Speciality Hospital is accredited by leading organizations for maintaining global standards in healthcare quality and patient safety. Key accreditations include:

  • JCI (Joint Commission International): Certified for delivering international standards of medical care.
  • NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers): Accredited for exceptional quality and patient safety protocols.
  • NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories): Certified for excellence in diagnostic services.
