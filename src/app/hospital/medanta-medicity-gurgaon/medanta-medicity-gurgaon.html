Established in 2009, Medanta The Medicity is a flagship super-specialty hospital that stands as a benchmark of excellence in healthcare. Spanning over 43 acres in Gurugram, the hospital is meticulously designed to deliver high-quality medical care with a strong emphasis on innovation, research, and education. Medanta integrates the latest medical technology with a patient-centered approach, providing a seamless healthcare experience. The hospital is home to some of the most renowned medical experts in the country and is equipped to handle the most complex medical cases.

Recognized as a leader in healthcare, Medanta has gained global acclaim for its clinical expertise, modern infrastructure, and compassionate care. With a strong focus on multidisciplinary treatment and cutting-edge procedures, Medanta has emerged as a trusted healthcare destination for patients from across India and the world.

Specialties and Services:

Medanta offers an extensive range of specialties, ensuring comprehensive care under one roof. Its core areas of excellence include:

  • Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery: Advanced heart care with innovative techniques, including robotic-assisted surgeries.
  • Oncology: Comprehensive cancer treatment with specialized centers for radiation, surgical, and medical oncology.
  • Neurology and Neurosurgery: Expertise in treating brain, spine, and nervous system disorders using advanced technology.
  • Orthopedics: Joint replacement, sports injury management, and trauma care led by highly skilled surgeons.
  • Gastroenterology and Hepatology: Expert care for digestive, pancreatic, and liver diseases.
  • Transplant Program: Globally recognized for successful organ transplants, including liver, kidney, heart, and lung transplants.

Facilities and Technology:

Medanta boasts world-class infrastructure and state-of-the-art facilities, such as:

  • Over 1,600 beds, including specialized ICU units.
  • Advanced robotic surgical systems and minimally invasive technologies.
  • Comprehensive diagnostic services and imaging facilities.
  • Dedicated rehabilitation and physiotherapy centers for post-operative recovery.

Accreditations and Certifications:

Medanta is accredited by prestigious organizations, ensuring adherence to global standards of healthcare quality and patient safety. Key accreditations include:

  • NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers): Recognized for providing high-quality patient care.
  • NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories): Accredited for excellence in laboratory services.
  • JCI (Joint Commission International): Internationally certified for delivering world-class medical services.
