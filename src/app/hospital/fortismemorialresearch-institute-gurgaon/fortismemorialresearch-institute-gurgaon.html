Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI), Gurgaon, is a flagship multi-super specialty hospital of Fortis Healthcare, established as a center of medical excellence in India. Spread across 11 acres, FMRI combines cutting-edge technology, innovative treatment approaches, and compassionate care to deliver world-class healthcare. Located in Gurgaon, this quaternary care hospital has earned a reputation as a trusted destination for advanced medical treatments, both in India and globally.

Fortis Gurgaon boasts a team of internationally trained medical experts across various specialties, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and diagnostic capabilities. The hospital emphasizes patient-centric care, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention and the highest standards of treatment. With its focus on clinical excellence and research-driven medical advancements, FMRI is at the forefront of healthcare innovation.

Specialties and Services:

Fortis Gurgaon offers a wide spectrum of medical specialties, ensuring comprehensive care across all disciplines. Key areas of expertise include:

  • Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery: Advanced treatments for heart conditions, including robotic-assisted and minimally invasive surgeries.
  • Oncology: Comprehensive cancer care with specialized centers for radiation, medical, and surgical oncology.
  • Neurology and Neurosurgery: Expertise in treating complex brain, spine, and nervous system disorders using cutting-edge techniques.
  • Orthopedics and Joint Replacement: Advanced solutions for bone, joint, and spine conditions, including robotic joint replacement surgeries.
  • Gastroenterology and Hepatology: Expert care for digestive, pancreatic, and liver disorders.
  • Transplant Program: Recognized for successful transplants, including liver, kidney, and bone marrow transplants.

Facilities and Technology:

FMRI is equipped with world-class infrastructure and cutting-edge technology to ensure precise diagnosis and effective treatments. Key features include:

  • Over 1,000 beds, including specialized ICUs and high-dependency units.
  • Robotic surgery systems and advanced minimally invasive procedures.
  • State-of-the-art diagnostic and imaging facilities, including PET-CT, MRI, and endoscopy suites.
  • Comprehensive rehabilitation and wellness centers for post-treatment recovery.

Accreditations and Certifications:

Fortis Memorial Research Institute adheres to the highest standards of healthcare quality and safety, recognized through prestigious accreditations:

  • JCI (Joint Commission International): Certified for delivering international standards of medical care.
  • NABH (National Accreditation Board for Hospitals and Healthcare Providers): Accredited for quality healthcare services and patient safety.
  • NABL (National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories): Certified for excellence in diagnostic and laboratory services.
